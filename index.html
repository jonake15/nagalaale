Welcome to Nagalaale Foundation

Nagalaale Foundation is a community-oriented charity focused on catalyzing community self-led initiatives aimed at uplifting livelihood standards through maximizing the available resources. The charity achieves this through nurturing individual potential and triggering community endeavors for the common good.

Read More

Strategic Direction 2023 - 2028

Over the next five years, Nagalaale Foundation will operate along a strategic direction inspired by two symbiotically connected goals; one focused on the internal institutional capacity and the other on the transformation of Kyaggwe and Bugerere communities.

    Mission

    The mission of Nagalaale Foundation is to trigger and catalyze transformative self-led initiatives in education and sports, healthcare, and livelihoods within communities.

    Vision

    Nagalaale Foundation envisions a community in which the African value of the common good is paramount and individual potential natured for optimum productivity.
